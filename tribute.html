Thelma "Tee" Dike is a Senior Product Designer and aspiring Tech Operator known for solving complex problems in high-stakes environments — translating technical systems into intuitive experiences that drive growth and retention.

Her career spans Flutterwave, Chipper Cash, battery engineering at Apple, and AI infrastructure work with Verizon R&D + Nvidia. Today she teaches AI in secondary schools and is writing a book on AI for teenagers — building not just products, but futures.

"From craft-focused design to operational leadership — a shift that defines her legacy."

Lessons I Carry Forward

Wisdom That
Will Travel Far.

These aren't just lessons learned — they are a compass. A way of moving through the world, through careers, through challenges. You gave us a framework for how to live and lead.
01

Think Like a Builder

Every product is a system. Every decision ripples outward. Approach design not as decoration, but as construction — laying foundations for something that lasts beyond you.

02

Design With Intention

There is no neutral choice in design. Each element communicates, guides, and shapes experience. Choose with purpose — every pixel, every word, every interaction carries meaning.

03

Lead With Courage

Leadership is not a title — it is a posture. Speak up, take up space, and bring others with you. True impact multiplies when shared generously with the next generation.
